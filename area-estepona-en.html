Why Estepona keeps growing

Known as the "New Golden Mile", Estepona is undergoing unprecedented transformation. Its charming historic old town, filled with flowers and cobblestone streets, coexists with a new first-class residential offer attracting buyers and investors from across Europe.

The arrival of modern infrastructure, new luxury golf courses and its proximity to Gibraltar have driven up demand for holiday rentals, with high-net-worth guests seeking quality and authenticity.

Vellum Costa has been in Estepona for years, anticipating market trends and positioning our clients' properties to capture the best of this boom before the competition arrives.

Is Estepona a good market for holiday rentals?

Yes. Estepona is one of the fastest-growing markets on the Costa del Sol. Its quality residential offering, renowned historic old town and proximity to Gibraltar make it a high-demand destination with very attractive occupancy rates.

What areas of Estepona do you manage?

We operate in all areas of Estepona: New Centre, Selwo, La Resina, Cancelada, El Paraíso and the Old Town, with deep knowledge of each micro-market and its specific demand patterns.

How much can my property earn in Estepona?

Estepona offers excellent returns, especially for modern properties with sea views or near the new town centre. The gross yield of a well-managed holiday rental can exceed 6–8% per year. Request our free audit for specific figures.
