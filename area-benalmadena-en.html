Spain's most beautiful Puerto Marina

Benalmádena is the gateway to the Costa del Sol for many travellers, just 20 minutes from Málaga Airport. Its Puerto Marina, considered one of the most beautiful in Spain with over 1,000 berths, is the social and gastronomic epicentre of the area.

The town has three very distinct zones: Benalmádena Costa (beach and marina), Arroyo de la Miel (residential with excellent transport links) and Benalmádena Pueblo (a historic hillside village). This diversity creates investment opportunities for every profile.

Vellum Costa provides comprehensive management that leverages Benalmádena's proximity to the airport, the vibrant life of Puerto Marina and the town's accessibility to generate a consistent and solid income stream for your property.

Benalmádena's logistical advantage: close to the airport, close to everything

Just 20 minutes from Málaga International Airport, Benalmádena has a competitive edge that very few municipalities on the coast can offer: easy arrival for guests. International travellers who arrive late or depart early especially value this proximity, and many make it a key selection criterion when booking. For owners, this means greater check-in flexibility and access to a wider, more diverse audience.

Puerto Marina and Benalmádena Costa: high demand for short and medium holiday stays

Properties around Puerto Marina and in Benalmádena Costa benefit from some of the most active holiday rental demand on the Málaga coastline. The marina's atmosphere, gastronomy and seafront promenade turns any stay into a complete experience. Sea-view or marina-view apartments command very competitive nightly rates, especially in spring and autumn, when prices ease but demand for quality remains strong.

Answers to your questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Benalmádena profitable for holiday rentals?

Yes. Benalmádena offers excellent ROI thanks to its combination of Puerto Marina, beaches, Tívoli World and easy access from Málaga Airport. Well-located properties achieve very high occupancy in peak season and solid demand throughout the rest of the year.

What is the difference between Benalmádena Costa and Arroyo de la Miel?

Benalmádena Costa is the beach area with the highest short-stay holiday demand. Arroyo de la Miel is more residential, with high long-stay demand from workers and families settling in the area. Both offer good returns but with different tenant profiles.

Do you manage apartments near Puerto Marina?

Yes. Puerto Marina and its surroundings are one of our most active management zones in Benalmádena. The marina's proximity, gastronomy and vibrant atmosphere make these properties a very attractive product for the holiday rental market.
