Why Marbella is unique

Marbella is undoubtedly the most recognised destination in southern Europe. The Golden Mile, Puerto Banús and Nueva Andalucía attract international demand that knows no season: high-net-worth clients from Scandinavia, the UK, the Middle East and Latin America seek quality properties throughout the year.

This mature, competitive market demands flawless management. A poorly managed property loses visibility on platforms and stops generating income. Vellum Costa knows every corner of Marbella and applies specific strategies so your asset stands out.

We manage everything from apartments in the old town to villas in the most exclusive developments, always with the same level of excellence and attention to detail that your guests expect.

Holiday rentals in Marbella: management for every type of property

Whether you own a two-bedroom apartment near the old town or a standalone villa in Sierra Blanca or Los Monteros, there is a guest profile looking for exactly your property. Demand in Marbella spans a very wide spectrum: European families seeking pools and comfort, couples drawn by gastronomy and culture, and groups travelling for golf or sailing. Professional management identifies that ideal audience and optimises your listing to attract them.

How much can a well-managed property earn in Marbella?

Market data is consistent: properties with a dynamic pricing strategy and optimised listings achieve average occupancy above 75% per year, peaking above 85% in summer and Easter. Even out of season, long-stay demand from digital nomads and temporary European residents keeps calendars active. Actual returns depend on your property's location and condition; our free audit gives you concrete figures.
