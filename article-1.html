Why does Estepona lead profitability growth in 2026?

October 2025 | Vellum Costa Team | 4 min read

Over the last decade, Marbella has been the undisputed epicenter of the Costa del Sol real estate sector. However, data from recent quarters reveals a paradigm shift: Estepona's "New Golden Mile" is offering excellent and sustained performance for property owners.

1. The saturation of mature areas vs. The expansion of Estepona

The real estate market in traditional municipalities, although extremely solid, has reached a point of maturity where the barrier to entry for new investors is very high. Estepona, on the other hand, has executed a smart urban development plan over recent years. The pedestrianization of its historic center and the construction of first-class infrastructure have attracted quality tourists seeking an authentic and peaceful experience without giving up top-tier services.

2. Occupancy Data and ADR (Average Daily Rate)

According to Vellum Costa's internal analysis cross-referenced with data from leading platforms, we observe two key trends:

  • Reduced seasonality: While other areas see their occupancy drop in winter months, Estepona maintains a more stable demand thanks to "Golf Tourism" and professionals and digital nomads from Northern Europe.
  • ADR Growth: The average price per night in well-managed properties (both modern apartments and villas) in Estepona has experienced very positive growth, consolidating it as one of the most profitable areas in the Malaga province.
"An owner in Estepona no longer competes solely on price, they compete on quality. The key is professional management to optimize every reservation."

3. The critical role of Revenue Management

Owning a property in Estepona is only 50% of the equation. The other 50% is how it is marketed. Owners who keep a flat rate throughout the high season are letting a significant part of their income slip away. At Vellum Costa, we apply daily Revenue Management strategies: if we detect a spike in demand due to a golf tournament or a local event, rates are dynamically adjusted to make the most of that opportunity.

Conclusion

Estepona has consolidated itself as a very smart destination for vacation rental investment in 2026. However, to maximize that return, traditional management is not always enough. It requires great visual presentation, an active pricing strategy, and exceptional guest care.
