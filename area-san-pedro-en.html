The authentic charm of the Costa del Sol

San Pedro de Alcántara is the authentic gem between Marbella and Estepona. Its Boulevard — an urban promenade lined with restaurants, terraces and local life — has transformed this town into a coveted destination for families and long-stay residents who seek an escape from mass tourism.

The combination of established residential estates like Guadalmina, coastal areas and a town centre with full services creates a diverse and consistent demand throughout the year, not only in peak season.

Vellum Costa harnesses this sustained demand to generate stable income for owners, with agile and hands-on management that maximises occupancy without compromising guest quality.

Property management in San Pedro: solid returns without relying on the luxury label

San Pedro de Alcántara offers something few areas can guarantee: solid, sustained profitability throughout the year, backed by genuine demand from families, sports enthusiasts and workers who choose the area for its quality of life. It does not rely exclusively on peak-season weeks; it has a loyal and recurring guest base that values fair pricing, comfort and proximity to full services.

The San Pedro Boulevard as a tourism and lifestyle hub

The famous San Pedro de Alcántara Boulevard — an urban promenade of over a kilometre lined with restaurants, terraces and leisure areas — has transformed the town centre into one of the most attractive walking destinations on the Costa del Sol. Properties within easy walking distance of this axis have a clear competitive edge on rental platforms, where guests actively seek the combination of beach, amenities and genuine local life.

Answers to your questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is San Pedro de Alcántara a good area to invest in?

San Pedro combines the charm of an authentic Andalusian village with modern infrastructure and its famous Boulevard. Its location between Marbella and Estepona, diverse residential offering and consistent demand from families and tourists make it one of the best price-to-yield areas on the Costa del Sol.

What is the most sought-after property type in San Pedro?

2- and 3-bedroom apartments near the Boulevard or a few minutes from the beach have the highest holiday rental demand. Family-friendly urbanisations with communal pools such as Guadalmina Alta or La Campana also achieve excellent occupancy.

Do you manage properties in Guadalmina?

Yes. Guadalmina — both Alta and Baja — is one of our main management zones in San Pedro de Alcántara. We know its demand characteristics and seasonality in depth.
