Panoramic views and privacy

Two worlds, one flawless management

Mijas is effectively two distinct markets. Mijas Pueblo, the picturesque white village in the hills, attracts tourists from around the world and a cultural guest profile that values authenticity and 180° views over the Mediterranean. Its properties command high rates throughout the year.

Mijas Costa — La Cala, Calahonda, Las Lagunas — is a beach and golf market with strong demand from families and international tourism, particularly British and Scandinavian, seeking well-located apartments and villas at competitive prices.

Vellum Costa understands both micro-markets and tailors its pricing, marketing and management strategy to each, maximising your property's performance regardless of its location within the municipality.

La Cala de Mijas: sustained growth and international demand all year round

La Cala de Mijas has established itself as one of the coastal destinations with the best value for money on the Costa del Sol. Its lively village centre, tranquil beaches and proximity to first-class golf courses attract a predominantly British, Irish and Scandinavian clientele that returns year after year. For owners, this translates into a loyal booking base that reduces dependence on peak-season spikes.

Mijas Pueblo: the unique appeal of views and authenticity

Properties in Mijas Pueblo play in a different league. The 180° panoramic views of the Mediterranean, the traditional white village architecture and the peaceful atmosphere attract guests who prioritise experience over price. Village houses, apartments with terraces and villas with gardens command above-average nightly rates when well presented and managed by someone who genuinely knows the territory.

Answers to your questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Mijas Costa and Mijas Pueblo for holiday rentals?

Mijas Costa (La Cala, Calahonda, Las Lagunas) has high beach demand and is ideal for peak-season and long-stay holiday rentals. Mijas Pueblo attracts a more cultural and exclusive guest profile, with characterful properties and spectacular 180° panoramic views that justify higher rates year-round.

Do you manage properties in La Cala de Mijas?

Yes. La Cala de Mijas offers one of the best price-to-occupancy ratios on the Costa del Sol. We manage apartments and houses throughout La Cala and the entire Mijas Costa coastline with in-depth knowledge of the local market.

How is maintenance handled for properties in the Mijas hills?

Properties in the upper areas of Mijas require special attention to pools, gardens, access roads and water systems. We work with specialists experienced in maintaining hillside and country properties in the area.
