Real transparency: What exactly happens when we inspect your property?

April 2026 | Vellum Costa Management | 4 min read

At Vellum Costa, we know that entrusting the keys to your home to someone else, especially when you are hundreds of kilometers away, requires enormous peace of mind. Whether you have a cozy apartment or a family home, the concern is usually the same: "Will everything be well taken care of? Will I find surprises on my bill?"

Today we want to open our doors and show you how we conduct our control inspections. Our goal is not only to keep your property impeccable, but to do so with absolute transparency.

The eyes of the guest (and the owner)

When our team enters your property, we don't just do a quick visual check. We walk through the space as if we were the next guest about to stay, but with the critical eye of the owner. We verify that the atmosphere feels fresh, the lighting is correct, there are no flickering bulbs, and the furniture is exactly where it should be. Taking care of a home means paying attention to the details.

Detecting "invisible issues"

Often, a property's biggest problems do not make noise. During our regular reviews we analyze what cannot be seen at first glance:

  • We check the condition of the air conditioning filters before the high season.
  • We check the battery level of smart locks and remote controls.
  • We inspect the back of appliances to ensure there are no small leaks.

Detecting a small water leak today costs very little; waiting for it to turn into a flood can significantly reduce your profitability.

"Our commitment is to care for your property with the same level of standards as if it were our own, prioritizing your peace of mind above all else."

Total control and prior approval (No surprises)

This is our greatest differentiating value. If we detect a failing appliance or a blind that needs fixing, we never make spending decisions behind your back.

Instead of directly calling a technician and passing the bill on to you later, we prepare a detailed report. This report includes clear photographs or videos of the problem. Only if you review the information and give us your express approval, we will proceed to contact the technician. You have financial control at all times.

The Digital Memory of your home

Every inspection, photo sent, and approved repair makes up the "health" history of the house. We keep an organized record that will allow you to know, for example, when a wall was last painted or the exact date a key element of your property was replaced.

Are you looking for this peace of mind for your property?
