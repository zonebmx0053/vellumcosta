The private retreat of the Costa del Sol

Benahavís is the municipality with the highest per capita income in Spain. Nestled in the mountains above Marbella, it is home to legendary estates such as La Zagaleta — Europe's most exclusive private community — alongside Los Arqueros and Monte Halcones. Here, real estate is not measured in square metres but in privacy, views and quality of life.

Properties in Benahavís attract ultra-high-net-worth tenants who demand total discretion and flawless service. This is not a volume market — it is a value market: fewer stays, higher rates and an experience that justifies every euro.

Vellum Costa manages these properties with the same mindset: quality over quantity, and a bespoke service protocol tailored to each owner and each guest.
