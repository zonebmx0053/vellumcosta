Cookie Policy

At Vellum Costa, we value transparency and direct communication. Therefore, we want to clearly explain how we use cookies on our website to improve our service for apartment and house owners on the Costa del Sol.

What are cookies?

They are small text files that are saved in your browser when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences (such as language) and understand what information is most useful to you.

What types of cookies do we use?

  • Technical Cookies (Essential): These are strictly necessary for the website to function properly and to remember, for example, whether you have already accepted or rejected this notice.
  • Analytical Cookies: These allow us to anonymously measure the number of visits to know which blog articles or services are of most interest to property owners.
  • Marketing Cookies: We use these to be able to show you relevant information about our property management services in a personalized way if you have shown prior interest.

How to manage your cookies

You have full control. You can allow, block, or delete the cookies installed on your device by configuring your browser options (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.). Please note that if you disable some cookies, the website may not function as smoothly.
