Cookie Policy

At **Vellum Costa**, we aim to be transparent about how we handle your privacy. This website uses cookies so we can understand which parts of our site are most helpful to property owners.

Which cookies do we use?

  • Technical Cookies: Essential for the website to function properly (e.g., to remember your cookie preferences).
  • Analytical Cookies (Google Analytics): These help us understand how many people visit us, without identifying them personally.

How to disable them?

You can restrict or block cookies through your browser settings at any time. However, this may affect your experience on our website.
