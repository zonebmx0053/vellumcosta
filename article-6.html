Vacation market forecasts for the high season

March 2026 | Vellum Costa Analysis | 4 min read

The Vellum Costa analysis team cross-references flight booking and hotel occupancy data to anticipate a highly dynamic summer. Everything indicates that the upcoming high season will be very positive for quality vacation rentals on the Costa del Sol.

Anticipation is key

We observe a constant shift in consumer behavior: "early booking" continues to grow. Forward-thinking guests no longer wait until May to plan their summer; they are closing deals during the first months of the year to secure the highest-rated properties.

Pricing and Service Trends

Our projections suggest that demand will continue to push prices upward, especially in consolidating areas like Estepona or the Golden Mile. However, clients are increasingly well-informed. The properties that achieve a real increase in profitability will be those that offer added services (24/7 support, impeccable cleaning, local guides) that justify their rates against the competition.

"Success no longer depends solely on location, but on the value and experience provided to the guest from the first point of contact."

Highest performing areas

While central Marbella maintains its classic status, we detect growing interest in residential complexes in quieter areas and independent properties along the entire west coast, where comfort and space are decisive factors for families.
