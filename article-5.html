The importance of 'Home Staging' in visual presentation

November 2025 | Vellum Costa Analysis | 4 min read

Why investing in visually preparing the house before taking photos can significantly improve annual performance. The concept of 'Home Staging' goes far beyond simple decoration. It is a real estate marketing tool designed to present a property in a way that attracts the maximum number of potential guests, offering not just a space, but an attractive and comfortable experience.

The psychology behind the booking

When a guest looks for accommodation on the Costa del Sol, they are not looking for a family home full of personal items or an excessively marked style. They are looking for a place to feel comfortable and relaxed. Home Staging depersonalizes the space and neutralizes it with a careful and professional touch, allowing the client to visualize themselves enjoying the property.

Return on Investment (ROI)

Many owners view Home Staging as an unnecessary expense. However, industry data confirms that it is one of the most effective strategies:

  • Increased visibility: Properties with a careful staging receive a higher number of visits on vacation portals.
  • Rate optimization: A well-presented home conveys higher quality and allows for a more competitive daily rate.
  • Better results: "Staged" homes tend to rent faster, optimizing calendar occupancy.
"We are not simply furnishing a house; we are creating the perfect setting for your next guests' vacation."

The basics of Vellum Home Staging

Our methodology focuses on three fundamental pillars: Light, Space, and Texture. We use suitable textiles, recommend a neutral color palette, and optimize the furniture layout to maximize visual spaciousness and achieve the best photographic result.
